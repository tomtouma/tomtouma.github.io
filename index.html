Tom Touma

Robotics & Mechatronics engineer,
Inventor & Farmer

Hi, I'm Thomas! When I'm not at work, I'm on my farm with my squad (the puppies you see!), riding dirt bikes, helping grandma & grandpa, & maintaing hundreds of acres of farm land with my family.

I work at NASA JPL & Caltech. I love to tackle unsolved problems & employ an end to end systems approach to solving them. My work since late 2018 involves the following:

(1) concept formulation for novel & autonomous UAV’s, legged robots & mechatronics devices.
(2) their mechanical & electrical design.
(3) their embedded microcontroller software & high-level integration.
(4) their manufacture & field testing.
